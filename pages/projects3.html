Capstone Project 3

How are the supermarkets faring?

For the next module, we learnt a new data analytic tool, Microsoft Power BI! It is one of the widely used tool to generate interactive reports. It is also similar to Tableau, Qlik just to name a few (gotta learn these on my own!) One think I like about Power BI is the drill-through function. It is such a handy function that gives the user the ability to filter through selective or all the pages.

In this capstone I decided to tackle on the Retail industry, supermarkets. I got my dataset from Kaggle (as usual ;p). It is a supermarket chain located in USA and Mexico. Since it has many chains, as a business analyst for the company (Maven Market), I am interested to know how the supermarket branches are faring in terms of sales/revenue generated. By knowing this, I would also be able to make a proposal to the management/operation team on which products or location are bringing in the money. This would allow the company to reposition itself by expanding or closure of the supermarkets.

  • Business need: How to increase supermarket chain sales?
  • Tool used: MS Power BI
  • Data file type: CSV file
  • Source: Maven Market Dataset (Kaggle)

Data preparation
Data was loaded into Power BI where data cleaning, transformation and EDA were performed.

New columns/aggregated columns were made using DAX. DAX is similar to Excel's formulas. Visualizations were also done in BI and drill-through was used as well as a mean to link up all the pages in the report.
